# Style Guide

## Visual Direction
- Base palette: deep blacks and layered graphite surfaces.
- Supporting neutrals: soft grays for borders, copy, and supporting UI.
- Accent: a single cool cyan accent for calls to action and highlights.

## Typography
- Primary font: a modern geometric sans for premium headlines and body copy.
- Secondary font: a mono accent for technical labels and supporting data.
- Maintain strong spacing rhythm with wide section padding and restrained line lengths.

## Motion
- Prioritize subtle reveal, stagger, and parallax-lite interactions over noisy animation.
- Default transitions should feel smooth and direct with `easeOut`.
- Respect reduced-motion preferences in all reusable animation wrappers.
